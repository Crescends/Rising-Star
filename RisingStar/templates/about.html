My life story

I am an upcoming techno music artist known by the name CampFire who enjoys binge-watching Netflix, ziplining, and parkour. I grew up in a middle class neighbourhood. No one really cares about the middle class, its either the unfortunate poor or the elite rich. My father died when I was very young and that led to me being raised by a distant mother for a short period of time before she died from a stroke. Life was never the same since that fatal day because the only person who raised me and truly cared about me was gone. Finding comfort in music, I started experimenting with my music to create sounds leading me to my first hit song “Smoke from the ashes”. Now traveling the country by the alliance of CampFire I gained a friend and musical rival, Marshmello and we became inseparable.
