BIOGRAPHY

Ashes' story began in Crown Point, Indiana. At a young age he was always interested in the art of music production. At 8 he learned and mastered sound technology and two instruments; this was the begining of Ashes' passion for music. He started getting noticed with the public in early August in 2018 with the creation of his original song "Smoke from the Ashes" that gained over 1,ooo views on the same day. This was the begining of his fame and start in the music making career.
